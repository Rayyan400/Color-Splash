Color Splash

"Color Splash" is a visual concept or technique often used in art and photography where a portion of an image is kept in full color, while the rest is either black and white or in a desaturated tone. This contrast draws attention to the colorful area, creating a striking focal point that can evoke emotions, highlight certain details, or add an artistic flair to an otherwise muted scene.
